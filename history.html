Our History

A journey born from loss, shaped by love, and built on hope.

Manisha Mandir was founded in the year 1985 by Dr. Sarojini Agarwal — not as part of a government plan or social scheme, but as an emotional response from a mother’s heart filled with pain and compassion.

In 1978, Dr. Sarojini tragically lost her beloved daughter Manisha in a road accident. The incident changed her life forever. But instead of letting grief consume her, she chose to transform her sorrow into a source of hope for others.Dr. Agarwal began by giving shelter to two orphaned girls in just three rooms of her home. With time, support from society followed, and this humble beginning grew into a full-fledged institution that has cared for over 800 girls to date.

Our Purpose

Manisha Mandir is not just a shelter — it is a family. Every girl here receives:

  • Safety and love
  • Quality education
  • Vocational and life skill training
  • Mental and emotional support

A Place Where Every Girl is Not Just Raised, But Empowered to Shine
