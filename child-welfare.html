What We Do

Nurturing Childhoods, Building Futures

At At Manisha Mandir, child welfare lies at the heart of everything we do. We work tirelessly to rescue, shelter, nurture, and empower abandoned, orphaned, and at-risk girls, giving them a second chance at life filled with safety, love, and opportunity.

Each child who enters our home is welcomed not as a beneficiary, but as a daughter of the Manisha Mandir family. Many of these girls come from traumatic pasts — abandoned at birth, lost in poverty, or rescued from abuse. We help them heal emotionally, grow intellectually, and evolve socially, guiding them toward a future filled with dignity and independence.

Key Areas of Focus

The Foundation of Every Child’s Growth

At Manisha Mandir, we believe that true child welfare goes beyond just providing shelter. Our work focuses on every aspect of a child’s development — physical, emotional, educational, and moral.

01
Safe Shelter

A home where every child feels protected, loved, and cared for — 24/7.

02
Nutrition & Health Care

Balanced meals, regular medical check-ups, and emotional well-being support.

03
Education Support

Formal schooling, tuition assistance, and digital learning tools to ensure quality education.

04
Values & Life Skills

We instill moral values, discipline, hygiene, and essential life skills to help them grow into responsible citizens.
