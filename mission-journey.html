Our Mission

Our mission is to provide orphaned and abandoned girls with a loving home, quality education, and the life skills they need to become confident, self-reliant, and responsible citizens. We believe every girl deserves not just shelter, but a family, dignity, and a future full of hope.

At Manisha Mandir, we believe every girl deserves love, dignity, and opportunity. Our mission is to transform lives through:

  • Emotional support and a nurturing home environment
  • Formal education and academic growth
  • Vocational training for independence
  • Moral values and life skills for a strong foundation

Our Journey

Journey of a Mother's Dream

A small step in 1985, a giant leap for countless lives. A journey of faith, love, and unshakable commitment to every girl’s future.

1985

Founded with just two orphaned girls in three rooms of Dr. Sarojini Agarwal’s home. A mother's love laid the foundation of Manisha Mandir.

1990

With growing support, the organization moved to a dedicated campus, offering more space and facilities for the children.

2000 onwards

Dozens of girls completed their education and began careers as teachers, nurses, and social workers — stepping into society with confidence.

Today

Over 800 girls have found a safe home, education, and a future filled with dignity and opportunity.

A Place Where Every Girl is Not Just Raised, But Empowered to Shine
