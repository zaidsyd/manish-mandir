About Us

जहाँ हर बेटी को मिलती है पहचान, शिक्षा और प्यार।

We believe that every girl deserves love, care, and the opportunity to grow into a confident, independent woman. Founded in 1985 by Dr. Sarojini Agarwal in memory of her beloved daughter Manisha, our NGO is more than just a shelter — it’s a home, a school, and a family for orphaned and abandoned girls.

उम्मीदों की किरण, हर बेटी के लिए

Every girl deserves a future filled with dreams and dignity. We empower them with love, education, and opportunity.
